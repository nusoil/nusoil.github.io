Position Title: Laboratory Technician (chemical) (NSE-2021-1)

Position Description: The position is for Laboratory Technician (chemical) who will work closely with the company’s materials development team for the chemical synthesis of a hydrogel product. Candidate(s) will be involved actively in chemical synthesis and product characterization. Work activities include but not limited to:

  • Perform work in wet lab, including preparation of solutions and process wet chemistry reaction
  • Perform basic materials characterization
  • Carry out experiments independently after training
  • Maintain a safe and clean laboratory environment
  • Data entry and simple data analyses
  • Communicate results through meetings, emails, and progress reports to supervisors. 

Eligibility:

  • Candidate must possess at least Diploma or equivalent in Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, or Materials Science and Engineering
  • Experience in hands-on work in a wet chemistry lab including synthesis and characterization would be an advantage
  • Ability to summarize data and present results effectively
  • Detail-oriented with strong record-keeping, documentation and organization skills
  • Self-motivated and ability to work autonomously with minimal supervision

Number of Vacancies: 1

Application Deadline: 30th April 2021

Please Email your resume and a short cover letter with the title Laboratory Technician (chemical) (NSE-2021-1) to: NuSoil Pte. Ltd. (careers@nusoil.sg)


Position Title: Field Assistant/Intern (NSE-2021-2)

Position Description: The position is for field assistant(s)/intern(s) who will work closely with the company’s development team on the field testing of a novel hydrogel. Candidate(s) will be involved actively in field work and plant growth assessment. Work activities include but not limited to:

  • Implementation of field tests (in greenhouse and indoor farm)
  • Soil preparation and watering of plants
  • Maintenance/establishment of plant cultures
  • Harvesting and collection of field data (egs, shoot/root weight, leaf area, scoring of number of leaves/flowers/fruits), including photo-documentation
  • Data entry and simple data analyses
  • Development of SOP to build best practices for different type of crops

Eligibility:

  • Diploma holder or experience in field farming/indoor farming
  • Strong interest in agriculture work
  • Self-motivated and ability to work autonomously with minimal supervision

Number of Vacancies: 2

Application Deadline: 30th April 2021

Please Email your resume and a short cover letter with the title Field Assistant/Intern (NSE-2021-2) to: NuSoil Pte. Ltd. (careers@nusoil.sg)
